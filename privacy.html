Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GoldRock ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects the information you provide when you visit our website or submit a form. We take your privacy seriously and will never sell or misuse your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with our website or submit an inquiry, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Name and contact information you voluntarily provide
  • Property or transaction-related information you choose to share
  • Any other details you voluntarily provide through our contact forms

We may also collect basic technical information automatically, such as your browser type, IP address, and pages visited, through standard web analytics tools. This information is used only to improve our website and is never tied to your personal identity.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit solely to:

  • Contact you about your property and discuss a potential cash offer
  • Schedule a walkthrough or follow-up conversation
  • Send you relevant communications related to your inquiry
  • Improve our website and the experience we provide

We may use your contact information to follow up on your inquiry, provide updates related to your property, or share relevant information about our services. You may opt out of communications at any time by contacting us directly.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Ever.

We may use trusted third-party service providers to help operate our business and deliver your inquiry to our team, including:

  • Automation and communication platforms that route form submissions to our internal team
  • Email and messaging services used solely for internal notification purposes

These tools are used only to deliver your information to our team. They do not store, sell, or use your data for any other purpose. We may also disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our team or others.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by applicable law. When information is no longer needed, we take reasonable steps to delete or anonymize it.

You may request that we delete your information at any time by contacting us through our contact form.

5. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to understand how visitors use our site. This information is aggregated and anonymous — it helps us understand which pages are most helpful and how to improve the experience for homeowners like you.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Doing so will not affect your ability to use our website or submit a form.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Request that we correct any inaccurate information
  • Request that we delete your information from our records
  • Opt out of any future communications from us at any time

To exercise any of these rights, simply reach out through our contact form. We will respond within 5 business days.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Our forms are transmitted securely, and access to your information is limited to authorized team members only.

No method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we work hard to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies before submitting any personal information.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted information through our website, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact us:

  • Fill out our contact form →

If you have a concern, please reach out using any of the contact methods above and we will respond promptly.
