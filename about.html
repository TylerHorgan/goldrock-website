OUR STORY

We Built GoldRock Because Colorado Homeowners Deserved Better.

We've been in Colorado real estate long enough to see every trick in the book. The inflated offers that get cut in half after the inspection. The self-written contracts with no closing dates. The "cash buyers" who don't actually have cash. The agents who collect their commission and disappear.

We were done watching it happen. So we built something different. GoldRock exists to give Colorado homeowners a real alternative — one built on transparent contracts, honest offers, and people who actually pick up the phone.

We're the cash buyer you actually want to recommend to your mom.

THE TEAM

Meet Tyler & Riley

When you work with GoldRock, you're not dealing with a call center or a faceless corporation. You're talking directly to the people buying your home.

Tyler
Founder

Tyler moved to Colorado from Richfield, Utah — a small town that taught him two things: your word is everything, and you either show up or you don't. He arrived in 2018 with a background in personal training and a reputation for being the person people call when things go sideways. That reputation followed him into real estate — and eventually became the foundation of GoldRock.

When Tyler was 19, his family lost their home to foreclosure. His mom filed for bankruptcy. His dad lost his business. He watched everything fall apart at once — and there was no one there to tell them there was another way out. He's never forgotten that. GoldRock exists because Tyler knows firsthand what it feels like to be in a distressed situation with no clear path forward — and he refused to let that be someone else's story if he could do something about it.

Facing foreclosure. Going through a divorce. Inherited a home you don't know what to do with. Whatever it is — he's been in that room before.

Some people are good to have around when things are easy. Tyler is the one you want when they're not — when you're behind on payments, going through a split, or just need out fast with no idea where to start. When everything feels like it has no good answer, you need one person who stays steady. Tyler is that person.

He's the 3AM phone call. The one who stays calm, tells you the truth, and figures it out when everyone else gives up. The people who know him best will tell you the same thing: when it gets bad — worst day of your life, no idea what to do — you call Tyler. He figures it out. Every time.

HOW WE OPERATE

The GoldRock Standard

These aren't marketing bullets. They're the actual principles we run every single deal by.

📋

CREC Approved Contracts. Always.

We use Colorado Real Estate Commission approved contracts on every deal. Not a self-written document. A real, state-approved contract you can hand to any attorney in Colorado.

💵

Real Offers. No Bait & Switch.

We do our homework before we walk through your door. The number we offer on day one is the number we close on. No inflated offers designed to get you under contract.

⚡

We Move Fast. Because You Need It.

Offer within 24-48 hours of the walkthrough. Close in as little as 7 to 14 days. We understand that when someone needs to sell, time is not a luxury they have.

🤝

Transparency Over Everything.

We'll tell you exactly how we calculate our offer — including what we factor in on our side. If our offer doesn't work for you, we'll tell you honestly and point you in the right direction.

📍

Local. Accountable. Reachable.

We live here. We invest here. Our reputation is built one deal at a time in the communities we operate in. If something goes wrong, you know exactly where to find us.

🛡️

Earnest Money at Title. Period.

Your earnest money goes straight to a licensed Colorado title company — never in our pocket. Clear release conditions, full transparency, no surprises at the closing table.
